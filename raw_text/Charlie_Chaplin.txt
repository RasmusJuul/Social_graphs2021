[[Thomas Shelby]] takes [[Grace Burgess]] to see him in person because Thomas knows his bodyguard, Wag MacDonald. Charlie Chaplin is a Romany Gypsy who was born on the Black Patch, a Gypsy camp in Birmingham. Chaplin is portrayed by Robert Elkin.
