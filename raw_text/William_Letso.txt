{{Infobox_characters|Title = William Letso|Image = William Letso.jpg|Series = [[Series 3|3]]|First = [[Episode 3.5]]|Last = [[Episode 3.6]]|Appearances = 2 episodes|Status = [[:Category:Status: Alive|Alive]]|Actor = [[Peter Bankole]]}}'''William Letso '''is a former diamond miner, a tunneler from the South African Labor Corps, and friend of [[Thomas Shelby]]. Letso appears briefly in [[episode 3.5]] and [[episode 3.6]]. He helps Thomas dig a tunnel into the strongroom of [[Leon Petrovich Romanov|Grand Duke Leon Petrovich]] and [[Izabella Petrovna|Grand Duchess Izabella Petrovna]] from a nearby property guarded by [[Johnny Dogs]] and members of his tribe.
