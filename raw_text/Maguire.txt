'''Maguire''' is a member of the [[Irish Republican Army]]. He regularly drinks in The Black Swan Pub in Sparkbrook, England.

== Series 1 ==
With his friend and fellow [[Irish Republican Army]] member Ryan, he visits [[Thomas Shelby]] at [[The Garrison|The Garrison Pub]] to discuss the rumoured stolen guns. The two men tell Thomas that it is rumoured that the [[Peaky Blinders]] gang are in possession of the guns, and that they would be willing to pay the gang a large sum of money to have them.

Maguire also sings the song, "[[The Boys of the Old Brigade]]" to Thomas before leaving the pub with Ryan.
