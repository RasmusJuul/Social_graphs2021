'''Charlotte Murray''' a wealthy girl whom [[Michael Gray]] has a short affair with. 

== Character History ==

=== SERIES 3 · 1924 ===
Charlotte Murray attends [[Grace Shelby|Grace]] and [[Thomas Shelby|Thomas]]'s wedding. Michael gives her cocaine. She wants to come to Birmingham because it's dirty, she wants to sleep with him because she thinks he would be dirty. The two continue to see each other after the wedding.
[[File:Michael Gray Charlotte Murray.png|left|thumb|220x220px|[[Michael Gray]] and Charlotte at the abortion house]]
Michael later gets Charlotte pregnant. Charlotte doesn't want to keep the baby. Michael sets the appointment for the abortion. Michael meets with Charlotte last time at the abortion house and leaves her.
