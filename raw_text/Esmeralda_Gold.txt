== Character History ==

=== SERIES 4 ===
Aberama Gold daringly tried to buy the not-for-sale [[Charlie Strong's Yard|yard]] owned by [[Thomas Shelby]]'s uncle [[Charlie Strong]].  
[[File:Modest wager.png|left|thumb|220x220px|The 'modest wager'.]]
Thomas suggests a modest wager. Heads, Gold gets the yard. Tails, Thomas has sex with Gold’s daughter, Esmeralda. Aberama doesn't toss the coin and promises to use Thomas’s penny to buy a flower to place on his grave.
