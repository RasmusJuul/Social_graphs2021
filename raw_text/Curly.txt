}}'''Curly''' is a stable boy and ferryman who often works in [[Charlie Strong's Yard]]. [[Thomas Shelby]] tells [[May Carleton]] that Curly's the best horseman in England.

==Background<nowiki/>==
He walks with a trot, indicating a previous leg injury. He is also simple minded but despite this he maintains a vast knowledge of horses. 

=== Series 1 ===
Curly warns Thomas Shelby his newly bought horse has been cursed and will soon die. He seems very distressed. 

=== Series 2 ===

===== '''Two years later (1922)''' =====
In [[Episode 2.4]], Thomas calls Curly 'the best horseman in England' while he shows May his horse, Grace's Secret.

=== Series 3 ===

===== '''Two years later (1924)''' =====

== Relationships ==

=== Charlie Strong ===
Curly is a close friend and employee of [[Charlie Strong]]. He works as a stable boy in [[Charlie Strong's Yard|his yard]].

=== Thomas Shelby ===
Curly is a friend of [[Thomas Shelby]] and a fellow member of the [[Peaky Blinders]]

The two trained and cared for Grace's Secret, Thomas' race-horse.
